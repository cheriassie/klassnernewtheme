KLASSNER

INTERNATIONAL DISTRIBUTION COMPANY

Welcome to Klassner s.r.o, founded in 2009 in Prague, Czech Republic, an international wholesaler of electronic consumable goods such as smartphones, tablets, consoles, accessories, wearable electronics, TVs, kitchen appliances, coffee machines, lifestyle and ecosystem products and other electronic items. The main key to our success is the long experience of building strong and profitable relationships with all our partners around the world. By being flexible and constantly responding to market changes, we offer an exceptional service, fast delivery and competitive prices. Our aim is to remain a competitive and profitable company on the chosen market. Our team is focused on driving the company towards better performance, consistency and high customer satisfaction. (Our strongest points are Fitbit wearables, Beats Headsets, Xiaomi lifestyle/ECO‐system products (which we import directly from HK), Apple iPhones, iPads, Apple watches, Airpods and Macbooks, Withings Scales/Watches and many more.) At the same time, we are constantly searching for new connection and partnerships, and with other brands as well. Contact us if you are seeking the opportunity to become a partner and build up a strong business relationship in the area of selling to / buying from us in particular. Looking forward to doing business with you!
